Cardano Blockchain Certificates

Take your credential wherever you go!

We are developing a blockchain-based certification process that will allow you to share your achievements with future employers without retrieving records from us. Upon completion of your final exam, we will conduct an in-person interview. If you pass, we will issue a certificate via a smart contract executable through a Cardano wallet. This certificate is unique to you and published as a non-fungible token (NFT).
