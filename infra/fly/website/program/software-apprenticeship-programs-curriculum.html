Gain the Prerequisite Skills

Please review our open-source Foundry Foundations Curriculum on GitHub. It will help you build the required skills if you have no programming experience. It can also help build your business and supply chain acumen. Forward Deployed Engineers need to have both technical skills and expertise in business systems and networks. Be sure to check the course fees for each linked course and the estimated time commitment that is required. The linked courses are not provided by us. We do not generate any revenue, referral fee, or any other type of compensation from the course provider.

Get Certified

Once you have completed or already possess the prerequisite training use Palantir's official training platforms to complete your coursework and take your certification exams.

Ontologize also offers official training and certification including in person and proctored exams. The company is founded and run by ex-Palantir employees. They are the gold standard for Palantir Foundry training.

Get Help

Use the Palantir Developer Community to get help from Palantir employees and experts.

Access our Discord server for live 1:1 help from professional Foundry engineers. It's free and can be a huge help to get over hurdles.
